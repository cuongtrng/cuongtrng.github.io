Nguyen Trong Cuong

 

Cuong Trong Nguyen is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree in computer science and engineering from the the University of Aizu (UoA), Japan. He received the B.E. degree in electronics and communications from the Hanoi University of Science and Technology (HUST), Vietnam, in 2021, and the M.Sc. degree in in computer science and engineering from the UoA, Japan, in 2024. He is actively involved in research as a member of the Computer Communications Laboratory, UoA.
